Steam Gog service

About the project

This project is a training work on working with the Steam API, as well as technologies such as: Angular and Nest.js.

Instruction

Most of the service's functions will not be available to a user who is not authorized using their Steam profile. To log in, use the button in the upper right corner of the toolbar, or follow the link by clicking the "Login now" button in this window.

Future of the project & Prospects

The service will develop further, new features will be added, such as the ability to write comments to other users on Steam, viewing hidden profiles, viewing discounts and promotions for games in the Steam store, and much more.
